Banana Bread

Description

A delicious quick bread made with browned bananas and common pantry ingredients and optional add-ins to adapt to your own liking!

Ingredients

  • 3 large, ripe bananas (about 267g-395g)
  • 75 g butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 150g sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract/black walnut
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 200g flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven 350F and butter 8x4" loaf pan
  2. Mash the ripe bananas with a fork
  3. Melt butter and mix with bananas until combined
  4. Mix in baking soda and salt
  5. Stir in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract until smooth
  6. Mix in flour stop as soon as the flour has disappeared
  7. Pour batter in pan and bake for 50 minutes to an 1 hour
