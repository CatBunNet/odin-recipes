Banana Bread

Description

A delicious quick bread made with ripe bananas and common pantry ingredients. A tender crumb makes it hard to put this bread down. Enjoy warm out of the oven, but it's even better the next day. Plus optional add-ins, like walnuts, chocolate chips, or even fruit so you can adapt it to your own liking!

Ingredients

  • 3 large, ripe bananas (about 267g-395g)
  • 75 g butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 150g sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract/black walnut
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 200g flour
  • optional add-ins: nuts, chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven 350F and butter 9x5" loaf pan.
  2. Mash the ripe bananas with a fork.
  3. Melt butter and mix with bananas until combined.
  4. Mix in baking soda and salt.
  5. Stir in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract until mixed well.
  6. Mix in flour; stopping as soon as the flour has disappeared.
  7. Stir in nuts or any optional add-ins now.
  8. Pour batter in pan and bake for 50 minutes to an 1 hour. Check with a toothpick. No batter, but crumbs are okay
  9. Cool in pan for 15 minutes then remove to rack to finish cooling.
